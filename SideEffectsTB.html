Side Effects Associated With TB Care


TB treatments can cause side effects—problems that occur when treatment affects healthy tissues or organs. Ask your health care team what side effects you are likely to have. Information on how to prevent or manage these problems is explained below.

Learn about steps you can take, and supportive care you will receive, to feel better – if you have one of these side effects:

  • Anemia
  • Appetite Loss
  • Bleeding and Bruising (Thrombocytopenia)
  • Constipation
  • Delirium
  • Diarrhea
  • Edema (Swelling)
  • Fatigue
  • Fertility Issues in Boys and Men
  • Fertility Issues in Girls and Women
  • Hair Loss (Alopecia)
